<p><ul><li>De-Icing Systems</li><li>Anti-Icing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ice protection systems are crucial in aviation and aerospace to ensure safety and operational efficiency. De-icing systems remove existing ice from surfaces using methods like hot air or electro-thermal elements, typically activated during flight. In contrast, anti-icing systems prevent ice formation before it occurs, employing techniques like heated surfaces or chemical applications to create a barrier against ice accumulation. Both systems are essential for maintaining aircraft performance and safety in adverse weather conditions, catering to various market needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
