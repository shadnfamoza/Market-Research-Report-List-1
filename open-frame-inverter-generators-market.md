<p><p>Open-frame inverter generators are categorized into two main types: manual and electric. Manual inverter generators require users to start the machine using a pull cord, offering simplicity and portability, making them ideal for outdoor activities. In contrast, electric inverter generators feature an electric start mechanism, allowing for easier operation with the push of a button. Both types convert mechanical energy into electrical energy efficiently, but the choice between them often depends on user preferences for convenience versus traditional operation.</p></p>
