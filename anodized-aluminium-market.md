<p><ul><li>Nanoporous Type</li><li>Porous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anodized aluminum market can be categorized into nanoporous and porous types. Nanoporous anodized aluminum features a highly uniform, nanoscale pore structure, making it ideal for applications requiring enhanced surface area and chemical reactivity, such as catalysis and filtration. In contrast, porous anodized aluminum has larger, irregular pores, suitable for applications like decorative finishes and lightweight structural components. Both types leverage the benefits of anodization to improve corrosion resistance and enhance aesthetic qualities, serving diverse industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
