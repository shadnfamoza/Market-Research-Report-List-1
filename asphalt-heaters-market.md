<p><p>The asphalt heaters market comprises various heating technologies, including infrared, steam, electric, and others. Infrared heating utilizes radiant energy to warm asphalt quickly and evenly, making it efficient for surface repairs. Steam heating involves the application of steam to maintain optimal asphalt temperatures, ensuring proper workability. Electric heating offers a clean and controlled method, utilizing electric coils or mats to deliver consistent heat. Other technologies may include gas-fired systems or thermal blankets, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
