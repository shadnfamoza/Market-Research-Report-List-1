<p><p>Underground Coal Gasification (UCG) is a process that converts coal buried underground into synthetic gas (syngas), which can be used for energy production and chemicals. The market includes various gasification types: Steam Reforming uses steam to convert hydrocarbons, Partial Oxidation involves limited oxygen to produce syngas, Auto-Thermal Reforming combines both processes for efficiency, and Combined or Two-Step Reforming enhances syngas yield. Biomass Gasification transforms organic materials into syngas, promoting renewable energy solutions.</p></p>
