<p><p>The Next Generation Sequencers (NGS) market includes several key technologies. Sequencing by Synthesis (SBS) involves synthesizing DNA strands and detecting nucleotide incorporation in real-time. Ion Semiconductor Sequencing measures changes in pH associated with nucleotide incorporation, enabling rapid processing. Single-Molecule Real-time (SMRT) Sequencing observes the sequencing process of individual molecules, providing real-time insights. Additionally, the "Other" market category encompasses emerging and hybrid technologies, offering diverse capabilities for genomics applications and expanding the NGS landscape.</p></p>
