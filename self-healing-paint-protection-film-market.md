<p><strong>The Self-Healing Paint Protection Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Self-Healing Paint Protection Film market is segmented into three main types: PVC, PU, and TPU. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) offers cost-effective, basic protection but lacks flexibility. PU (Polyurethane) provides better durability and self-healing properties, offering enhanced protection against scratches and environmental factors. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) is known for its superior elasticity and resistance to abrasion and UV exposure, making it a premium option. These types differ in performance, flexibility, and protection levels, catering to various consumer needs.</p></p>
