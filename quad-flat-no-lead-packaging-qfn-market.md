<p><p>Quad-Flat-No-Lead (QFN) packaging is a compact, leadless semiconductor package designed for efficient thermal performance and reduced footprint. The market for QFN is primarily divided into two types: punched and sawn. Punched QFN involves creating packages through a stamping process, allowing for high volume and cost-effective production. Sawn QFN, on the other hand, is derived from sawing larger wafers into individual chips, offering more customization and flexibility. Both types cater to diverse electronics applications, enhancing device performance and integration.</p></p>
