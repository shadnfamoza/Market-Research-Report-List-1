<p><p>Single Board Computers (SBCs) are compact computing platforms designed for various applications, ranging from embedded systems to complex industrial tasks. Within the SBC market, specific types include cCPI (compact Computer Peripheral Interface), VME (Versa Module Europa), VPX (VITA Packet Exchange), and ATCA (Advanced TCA). Each type addresses different needs: cCPI focuses on small form factors, VME offers modular design, VPX is geared towards high-speed data handling, and ATCA supports telecommunications and network applications, highlighting versatility across sectors.</p></p>
