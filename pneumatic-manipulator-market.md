<p><ul><li>Hanging</li><li>Column</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic manipulator market can be categorized primarily into two types: hanging and column manipulators. Hanging manipulators are suspended from overhead structures, allowing for flexible movement and access to various areas, making them ideal for large workspaces. Column manipulators, on the other hand, are mounted on a fixed base, providing stability and precision for tasks that require a defined range of motion. Both types leverage pneumatic technology to assist workers in handling heavy or awkward loads efficiently and safely.</p></p>
