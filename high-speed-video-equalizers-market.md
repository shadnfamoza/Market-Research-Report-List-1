<p><p>High-speed video equalizers are essential components in signal processing for improving image quality by compensating for distortion and signal degradation. The market is divided into semi-parametric and fully-parametric equalizers. Semi-parametric equalizers use a combination of fixed and adjustable parameters to enhance video signals, offering a balance between complexity and performance. In contrast, fully-parametric equalizers provide complete customization of parameters, allowing for precise control over the signal characteristics and ensuring optimal video quality in advanced applications.</p></p>
