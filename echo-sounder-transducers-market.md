<p><ul><li>Split-beam Transducers</li><li>Single Beam Transducers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The echo sounder transducer market includes two primary types: split-beam and single-beam transducers. Split-beam transducers utilize multiple beams to enhance target detection and improve measurement accuracy, making them ideal for detailed assessments in various water depths. In contrast, single-beam transducers emit one beam for depth measurement, offering simpler and cost-effective solutions typically used for basic surveying and navigation. Both types cater to distinct applications in fisheries, oceanography, and environmental monitoring, reflecting diverse user needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
