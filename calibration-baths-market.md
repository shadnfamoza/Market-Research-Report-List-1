<p><strong>The Calibration Baths Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-Block</li><li>Liquid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Calibration baths are essential tools used to maintain accurate temperature measurements in various industries. There are two main types: dry-block and liquid calibration baths. Dry-block baths utilize a solid-state design that allows for rapid temperature stabilization and is less prone to contamination, making them ideal for field use. Liquid calibration baths, on the other hand, employ a liquid medium for heating and cooling, offering uniform temperature distribution, making them suitable for laboratory environments and large volume calibrations.</p></p>
