<p><ul><li>Chock Support</li><li>Shield Support</li><li>Chock Shield Support</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powered support market includes three main types: chock support, shield support, and chock shield support. Chock support provides adjustable props to support roof stability in mining operations, enabling controlled movement. Shield support features a robust framework offering comprehensive coverage and resistance against rock falls. Chock shield support combines elements of both, providing enhanced safety and stability by integrating the mobility of chocks with the protective characteristics of shields. Each type serves to optimize worker safety and operational efficiency in underground mining environments.</p></p>
