<p><p>The lithotripters market is categorized into several types based on the technology used to fragment kidney stones. Hydroelectric shock wave lithotripters utilize high-pressure water pulses to create shock waves that break stones. Electromagnetic shock wave lithotripters generate shock waves using electromagnetic fields, providing precise targeting of stones. Other types may include piezoelectric or laser lithotripters, each with distinct mechanisms and applications. The choice of lithotripter type influences treatment efficacy, patient comfort, and recovery times.</p></p>
