<p><ul><li>Single Vision Lenses</li><li>Progressive Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-fatigue lenses market encompasses two primary types: single vision lenses and progressive lenses. Single vision lenses provide a uniform prescription across the lens, suitable for those who require correction for either distance or near vision, reducing eye strain during prolonged tasks. Progressive lenses, on the other hand, offer a smooth transition between multiple focal points, accommodating distance, intermediate, and near vision in one lens, making them ideal for individuals who experience fatigue from frequent switching between different vision needs.</p></p>
