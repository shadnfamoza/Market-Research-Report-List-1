Fully automatic robots operate independently, performing tasks such as sample analysis, pipetting, and data management with minimal human intervention, enhancing efficiency and precision. In contrast, semi-automatic robots require some level of human oversight or intervention, making them suitable for laboratories that seek automation while still needing manual input for certain processes. Both types contribute significantly to improving research workflows in biological studies.</p></p>
