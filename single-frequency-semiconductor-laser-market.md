<p><p>The Single-frequency Semiconductor Laser market is divided into two main types based on feedback mechanisms: Intracavity Optical Feedback and External Cavity Optical Feedback. </p><p>Intracavity Optical Feedback lasers incorporate an optical feedback loop within the laser cavity, enhancing coherence and stability. In contrast, External Cavity Optical Feedback lasers utilize an external optical component, such as a grating or mirror, to refine the laser output, allowing for tunability and improved spectral purity. Both types are essential in applications requiring precise wavelength control and high stability.</p></p>
