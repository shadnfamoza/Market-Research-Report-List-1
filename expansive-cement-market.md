K Type expansive cement is designed for use in areas requiring minimal shrinkage and enhanced stability, making it suitable for repair works. M Type expansive cement offers moderate expansion and is often used in construction projects where volumetric stability is essential. S Type expansive cement provides higher levels of expansion, ideal for applications in severe shrinkage conditions, such as soil stabilization and controlled cracking.</p></p>
