<p><ul><li>Drive Hydraulic Wrench</li><li>Hollow Hydraulic Wrench</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic torque wrench market is primarily divided into two main types: drive hydraulic wrenches and hollow hydraulic wrenches. Drive hydraulic wrenches utilize a drive mechanism to generate high torque, suitable for various bolting applications, particularly in confined spaces. In contrast, hollow hydraulic wrenches feature a through-hole design allowing for the passage of a bolt or pipe, making them ideal for situations requiring simultaneous torque application while maintaining tool versatility. Both types enhance efficiency and precision in industrial settings.</p></p>
