<p><ul><li>Sensing Gyroscope</li><li>Indicating Gyroscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS gyroscope market is divided into two primary types: sensing gyroscopes and indicating gyroscopes. Sensing gyroscopes detect and measure angular velocity and are commonly used in smartphones, drones, and automotive applications for navigation and stabilization. Indicating gyroscopes, on the other hand, provide orientation information by measuring the angle of rotation, often utilized in aviation and robotics for precise navigation. Both types enhance motion tracking and control, driving advancements in various technological sectors.</p></p>
