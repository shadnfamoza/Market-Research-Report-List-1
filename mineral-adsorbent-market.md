Zeolites, with their crystalline structure, effectively capture ions and gases. Clay serves as a low-cost adsorbent in various industries, while silica gel absorbs moisture and odors, making it essential for packaging and storage. Other materials also contribute to specialized adsorption needs.</p></p>
