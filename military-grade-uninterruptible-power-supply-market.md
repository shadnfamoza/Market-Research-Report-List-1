<p><p>The Military Grade Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) market includes three primary types: Passive Standby, Online, and Line-Interactive. Passive Standby UPS systems switch to battery power only during outages, offering basic surge protection. Online UPS provides continuous power without interruption, ideal for sensitive military equipment, as it converts incoming AC to DC and then back to AC. Line-Interactive UPS combines features of both, allowing voltage regulation and conditioning while maintaining efficiency, making it suitable for environments with fluctuating power quality.</p></p>
