<p><p>The bicycle taillight market is primarily divided into two types: self-generating lights and battery-powered lights. Self-generating lights harness energy from the bike's movement or pedals, eliminating the need for batteries and providing a sustainable lighting option. In contrast, battery-powered lights rely on disposable or rechargeable batteries for operation, offering convenience and often more powerful illumination. Both types enhance cyclist visibility and safety, catering to different preferences regarding maintenance and environmental impact.</p></p>
