<p><p>The vacuum coating service market primarily includes two key types: Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) coating service and Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) coating service. PVD involves transforming solid materials into vapor and condensing them onto substrates, providing hard, durable coatings with excellent adhesion. In contrast, CVD entails chemical reactions to form a thin film on the surface, offering uniform coverage and high purity. Both methods are widely used across various industries for enhancing the performance and aesthetic qualities of products.</p></p>
