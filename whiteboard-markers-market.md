<p><p>The whiteboard markers market is primarily divided into two types: dry erase and wet erase markers. Dry erase markers are designed for quick, easy removal from non-porous surfaces, making them popular for everyday use in classrooms and offices. In contrast, wet erase markers rely on water-soluble ink and require a damp cloth for removal, making them ideal for more permanent applications on whiteboards, such as project planning or presentations. Both types cater to different user needs and preferences.</p></p>
