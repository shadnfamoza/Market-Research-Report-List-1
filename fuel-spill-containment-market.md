<p><p>The fuel spill containment market includes two primary types: Remote-Fill and Direct-Fill. Remote-Fill systems allow fuel to be transferred from a safe distance, minimizing exposure risks during operations and enhancing safety protocols. Conversely, Direct-Fill systems involve fueling activities taking place in closer proximity, often providing quicker refueling times but potentially increasing hazards. Both types prioritize environmental protection and compliance with regulations, addressing the need for effective spill management and containment strategies in various industries.</p></p>
