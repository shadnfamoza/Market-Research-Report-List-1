<p><p>The residential food dehydrator market is segmented into two main types: stacked trays and slide-in trays. Stacked tray dehydrators feature multiple trays layered on top of each other, allowing for efficient use of vertical space. They are typically more compact and suitable for small kitchens. In contrast, slide-in tray dehydrators have trays that pull out horizontally, making it easier to access and load food. This design allows for better airflow and uniform drying, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
