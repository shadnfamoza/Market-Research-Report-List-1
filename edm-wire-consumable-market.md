<p><p>The EDM wire market is categorized into three main types: No Coated Wire, Coated Wire, and Hybrid Wire. No Coated Wire is utilized for straightforward machining tasks, offering basic conductivity and performance. Coated Wire features a protective layer that enhances durability and reduces wear, making it suitable for more demanding applications. Hybrid Wire combines characteristics of both coated and non-coated wires, providing improved performance and versatility, catering to specialized machining needs in various industries.</p></p>
