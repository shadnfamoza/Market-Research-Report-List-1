<p><p>The High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) treatment market is primarily segmented into two types: ultrasound-guided and MR-guided. Ultrasound-guided HIFU utilizes real-time imaging via ultrasound to precisely target tissue, making it suitable for various applications like tumor ablation. MR-guided HIFU, on the other hand, employs magnetic resonance imaging for improved visualization, enhancing accuracy and safety during treatment. Both types cater to different surgical needs and preferences, influencing their demand and adoption in clinical settings.</p></p>
