<p><p>The Time-of-Flight (ToF) distance sensor market is categorized into three types based on measurement ranges. Close-range ToF sensors typically cover distances from a few centimeters to around one meter, ideal for applications like gesture recognition and proximity sensing. Medium-range ToF sensors operate between one to ten meters, suitable for robotics and industrial automation. Long-range ToF sensors exceed ten meters and are used in applications such as automotive detection, environmental mapping, and surveillance, providing greater accuracy over significant distances.</p></p>
