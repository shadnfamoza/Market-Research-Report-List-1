<p><p>The collagen biomaterial market is categorized into several types based on the source of collagen. Bovine collagen, derived from cows, is known for its structural properties and is widely used in medical and cosmetic applications. Porcine collagen, sourced from pigs, is favored for its similarity to human collagen, enhancing biocompatibility. Marine collagen, obtained from fish or other marine organisms, offers unique benefits such as lower allergenicity and is gaining popularity in nutraceuticals. The "Others" category includes collagen from alternative sources like plants or synthetic options.</p></p>
