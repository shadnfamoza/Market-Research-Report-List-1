<p><strong>The Aviation Propulsion Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-Breathing</li><li>Non-Air Breathing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aviation propulsion systems market is primarily divided into air-breathing and non-air-breathing systems. Air-breathing propulsion, including turbojets, turbofans, and turbochargers, utilizes atmospheric air for combustion, making it suitable for commercial and military aircraft. Non-air-breathing systems, such as rocket engines, operate in space or in vacuum conditions, relying on onboard oxidizers to facilitate combustion. Each type plays a critical role in different flight regimes, with air-breathing systems being predominant for subsonic and supersonic flights, while non-air-breathing systems are essential for space exploration.</p></p>
