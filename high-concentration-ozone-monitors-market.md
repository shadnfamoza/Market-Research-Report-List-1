<p><p>The High Concentration Ozone Monitors market is primarily categorized into two types: UV Absorption and Electrochemistry. UV Absorption monitors utilize ultraviolet light to detect ozone levels by measuring the difference in light absorption at specific wavelengths. This method is known for its accuracy and reliability in high ozone environments. On the other hand, Electrochemical monitors rely on chemical reactions that generate a current proportional to the ozone concentration. They are typically more portable and cost-effective, making them suitable for various applications.</p></p>
