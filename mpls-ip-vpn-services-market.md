<p><strong>The MPLS IP VPN Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Layer 2</li><li>Layer 3</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MPLS IP VPN services are categorized into Layer 2 and Layer 3 markets based on the network layer they operate at. Layer 2 MPLS VPN services provide point-to-point or multipoint connections, enabling customers to manage their own IP routing, suitable for businesses needing flexibility and control. Conversely, Layer 3 MPLS VPN services include routing functionality within the service provider’s infrastructure, simplifying management and offering built-in quality of service, which is ideal for users focusing on efficient data transmission without extensive routing overhead.</p></p>
