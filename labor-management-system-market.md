<p><p>The Labor Management System (LMS) market is segmented into two primary deployment types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise LMS involves installing software directly on a company's servers, offering greater control over data and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based LMS operates on remote servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs with ongoing subscription fees. Cloud solutions allow for easy access from various devices, fostering collaboration and real-time data utilization. Both deployment types cater to diverse business needs.</p></p>
