<p><p>Vacuum tray dryers utilize different heating mediums to facilitate drying under vacuum conditions. The thermic fluid type employs a heat transfer fluid for efficient and uniform heating. Steam-driven systems utilize steam for heating, ensuring rapid drying while maintaining product quality. Hot air dryers circulate heated air within the vacuum container, promoting moisture evaporation. Electric vacuum tray dryers use electric heaters, providing precise temperature control and energy efficiency. Each type serves specific industrial applications based on product requirements and operational efficiency.</p></p>
