<p><p>The explosion-proof solenoid valve market is categorized into three main types: two-way, three-way, and four-way solenoid valves. Two-way solenoid valves control the flow between two ports, commonly used for on/off applications. Three-way solenoid valves manage flow between three ports, enabling mixing or diverting functions, ideal for more complex systems. Four-way solenoid valves have four ports and are primarily used in actuating double-acting cylinders, providing versatile control in pneumatic systems while ensuring safety in hazardous environments.</p></p>
