<p><p>The Near Infrared (NIR) portable spectrometer market encompasses various types of analytical techniques. Atomic spectroscopy focuses on the identification of elemental composition through light interactions. Mass spectrometry analyzes the mass-to-charge ratio of ions for precise molecular identification. Molecular spectroscopy examines molecular vibrations and transitions, providing insights into molecular structure and concentration. Fluorescence spectroscopy detects light emission from molecules after excitation, allowing for sensitive and selective analyses. These techniques cater to diverse applications in environmental monitoring, food safety, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
