<p><strong>The PCB Laser Drilling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CO2 Laser Drilling System</li><li>UV Laser Drilling System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB laser drilling system market primarily includes two types: CO2 laser drilling systems and UV laser drilling systems. CO2 laser drilling systems utilize infrared laser beams, ideal for drilling larger holes in various materials, including non-metallic substrates, making them suitable for standard PCBs. In contrast, UV laser drilling systems operate at shorter wavelengths, providing high precision for microvias and intricate designs, particularly in high-frequency applications. Both technologies cater to the increasing demand for miniaturization and enhanced performance in electronics.</p></p>
