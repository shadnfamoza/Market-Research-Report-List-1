<p><p>The Gilding Machine market involves two primary types: Round Flattened Gilding Machines and Platen Flattened Gilding Machines. Round Flattened Gilding Machines are designed for more intricate designs, often facilitating the application of gold leaf onto irregular surfaces. In contrast, Platen Flattened Gilding Machines utilize a flat platen to apply gold leaf evenly and efficiently, ideal for flat surfaces. Each type caters to different production needs, enhancing the decorative finishing capabilities in various industries such as printing, packaging, and crafting.</p></p>
