<p><p>The rapid test market comprises several key technologies. PCR-based tests amplify DNA for quick pathogen detection, ensuring high specificity and sensitivity. Immunoassay-based tests utilize antibodies to identify specific antigens, making them ideal for diagnosing infections and measuring biomarkers. Chromatography-based tests separate components in a sample for analysis, offering robust results in various applications. Spectroscopy-based tests analyze light interaction with samples, providing rapid qualitative and quantitative data. Together, these methods enhance diagnostics and streamline disease management across healthcare settings.</p></p>
