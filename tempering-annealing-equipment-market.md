<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tempering annealing equipment is categorized into two primary market types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal tempering annealers are designed for continuous processing of materials, offering uniform heat distribution and efficient operation. They are ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, vertical tempering annealers cater to batch processing, supporting larger workpieces and specialized applications. This design allows for a more compact footprint and can enhance heating efficiency. Both types serve distinct needs in the metallurgy and manufacturing sectors, optimizing heat treatment processes.</p></p>
