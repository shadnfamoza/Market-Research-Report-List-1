<p><p>The dual element ultrasound probe market features two primary types: straight probes and angled probes. Straight probes are designed for direct, perpendicular imaging, offering high-resolution images in applications like vascular and cardiac studies. Angled probes, on the other hand, are configured to facilitate access to difficult anatomical regions and improve visualization in complex areas such as the abdomen and pelvis. Both types serve vital roles in medical diagnostics, enhancing the versatility and effectiveness of ultrasound imaging techniques.</p></p>
