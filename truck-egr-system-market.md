<p><ul><li>External EGR</li><li>Internal EGR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Truck EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) system market can be segmented into two main types: External EGR and Internal EGR. External EGR involves routing exhaust gases from the engine's exhaust manifold to the intake system, reducing nitrogen oxide emissions. It is typically used in larger truck engines for efficiency. Internal EGR utilizes exhaust gases that remain within the cylinder, enhancing combustion while minimizing emissions. Both systems aim to improve fuel efficiency and comply with stringent emissions regulations in the trucking industry.</p></p>
